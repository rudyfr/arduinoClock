33 Clock Object
34 ================
35 The Clock maintains state of the current:
36 - Day of Week
37 - Hour (in 24 hour format)
38 - Minute
39 - Second
40 - Ticks in Milliseconds at last update, which is less than or equal to the output of millis()
41 
42 Clock.run() updates the current time. If the clock is behind by more than a second, it only adds one second. This guarantees all time states are entered, however if the clock is run less than once per second, it will lose time.
